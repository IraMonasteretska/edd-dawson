Edd Dawson

Hi! I’m Edd, I’ve been involved in tech since graduating in Computer Science in the late 90’s and online marketing since the early 2000’s. I’ve built,run and sold a number of websites and businesses over that time, the most well known of those is broadband.co.uk.

I’d probably describe myself as primarily an Affiliate Marketer nowadays with a strong emphasis on SEO and CRO. I invest in and advise startups. I love to build teams and partnerships around new products and marketplaces. To learn more about what I’m experienced in check out the sections below
